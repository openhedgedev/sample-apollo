Professional Employer Organisations

Professional Employer Organisations (PEOs) play an increasingly important role servicing this growing trend. From Employer of Record services, to recruitment, to statutory filings, businesses require specialist HR assistance. This ensures that teams in new jurisdictions are brought into the business efficiently and compensated as agreed. For the corporate, at the very least it means legal compliance and all necessary protections. Central to the PEO's list of services is HR Operations. This includes important aspects such as onboarding, redundancies, benefits and expenses. However, payroll services is usually the key requirement. Managing a multi-country workforce of employees, contractors and service providers without the oversight of a PEO would quickly topple the economic benefits of such practices.

PEOs offer corporate customers a range of payroll services from invoicing through to complete solutions.
For example:

  • PEO periodically provides a list of payment instructions to client
  • PEO invoices kind for total payroll and distributes monies in local currencies
These multicurrency payments are made by the corporate periodically over time and are therefore means space susceptible to movements in currency prices.
